# Data Harbor Product Passport Cut

## Creative Reset

This cut intentionally abandons the previous dark-neon / robot-agent language.
The new metaphor is a product passport desk: evidence stamps, research routes,
ledger rows, and review paperwork becoming structured product data.

## Visual Identity

- Background: warm paper, visible grid, light editorial pacing.
- Primary colors: ink black, warm paper, red stamp, SEIDOR blue, evidence green,
  ledger gold.
- Typography: large editorial serif headlines with compact system-sans metadata.
- Agent metaphor: route lines, source chips, evidence stamps. No robot icons, no
  glowing droid blob, no sci-fi orb swarm.
- App footage: treated as proof inside framed operator-desk panels.

## Motion Rules

- Use fast paper wipes between scenes.
- Keep text entrances short and decisive.
- Use deterministic canvas route drawing for the research-agent moment.
- Do not use random motion, timers, mascot animation, or auto-playing timelines.
- Keep app video clips muted and controlled by HyperFrames timing.

## Story Beats

1. Product data ages faster than manual review.
2. Manual enrichment creates queue and revenue cost.
3. Data Harbor by SEIDOR reframes enrichment as an evidence workflow.
4. One launch sends research routes to trusted sources.
5. Evidence is mapped into a structured product passport.
6. Product list: select items and launch batch agents.
7. Product review: accept candidate changes with proof.
8. Governance: schemas and aggregators define source policy.
9. Final: ship product data with proof, not backlog.
